Casa / About

A quieter way to find a holiday stay.

Casa is a UK marketplace built for direct bookings — no guest service fees, no host commission, and a community feed where hosts and guests share real recommendations before you book.

Why we started

Holiday rental platforms grew fast by adding fees on both sides. Guests pay more at checkout; hosts lose a slice of every booking. Casa takes a different path: a flat £19/month listing fee for hosts, £0 booking fees for guests, and tools that keep the relationship between guest and host — not a black-box algorithm.

We launched in 2025 with a small team in London, focused entirely on the UK. From shepherd's huts in Cumbria to blackhouses on Skye, we wanted a site that feels as considered as the places it lists.

Fee-free for guests

No service charge at checkout. The price you see is what you discuss with the host — cleaning fees included upfront where listed.

100% for hosts

Hosts keep every penny of the nightly rate. Optional Casa Concierge handles first-line enquiries — your rules, plus your Casa calendar (including dates synced from Airbnb).

Community first

The feed lets hosts post availability, guests ask questions, and everyone share local tips — like asking a friend who knows the area.

What we believe

  • Transparency beats surprise fees — show the full stay cost before enquiry.
  • Hosts know their properties best; Casa should get out of the way.
  • Trust comes from verified profiles, honest reviews, and open conversation.
  • The UK has extraordinary places — they deserve photography and copy that do them justice.
