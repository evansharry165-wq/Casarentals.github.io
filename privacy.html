Privacy policy

Draft for beta · June 2026

Casa.co.uk (“Casa”) is a fee-free UK accommodation platform. This policy explains how we handle personal data when you browse, join the waitlist, list a property, or message hosts and guests.

What we collect

  • Account details: name, email, role (guest or host)
  • Listing and enquiry content you submit
  • Messages sent through Casa (including Casa Concierge when enabled)
  • Technical data: device, browser, approximate region (for analytics)

How we use it

To operate the platform, connect guests with hosts, send service emails, improve the product, and comply with law. We do not sell your data to third-party advertisers.

Casa Concierge

When a host enables Concierge, message content may be processed by automated systems to draft replies within the host’s stated rules. Guests are informed when Concierge is active. Hosts remain responsible for bookings and payments.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for sign-in sessions and preferences (e.g. saved listings). Analytics cookies are off by default in beta.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing privacy@casa.co.uk. You may complain to the ICO if you believe we have handled your data incorrectly.
