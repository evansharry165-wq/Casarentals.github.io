About the cottage

A 300-year-old stone cottage tucked into the fells just outside Windermere village. Three bedrooms, sleeps six, with original oak beams, a flagstone kitchen, and a wood burner that throws heat all evening. The garden steps down through wild ferns to a beck — there's a swing, a fire pit, and a view straight to Loughrigg Fell.

We bought the place in 2024 after years of holidaying in the Lakes and slowly bringing it back to itself — re-pointing the stonework, restoring the windows, and putting the heart back into the kitchen. It's been a labour of love. We hope you'll feel it.

Walk to the pub in 10 minutes. Windermere lake is 5 minutes by car. Dogs welcome (we have two of our own). Cot and high chair available on request.
