How do I book a stay?

Search by region and dates, open a listing, and send an enquiry. Casa charges £0 booking fees — you agree dates and payment directly with the host. Instant Book listings can be confirmed immediately; others receive a host reply within their stated response time.

How do I list my property?

Sign up as a host, complete the listing wizard, and pay the flat £19/month listing fee. No commission on bookings. In the dashboard, connect your Airbnb or Booking.com calendar export link once — Casa blocks those dates automatically.

Optional Casa Concierge handles first-line enquiries for £24/month or 2% on AI-confirmed bookings. The feed is optional: post from your calendar when you have a gap, not as a daily chore.

Double bookings? Import external calendars read-only. When you accept a Casa enquiry, those dates block on your Casa calendar too.

What does Casa cost?

Guests: £0 service fees at checkout.
Hosts: £19/month per listing, 0% commission. You keep 100% of the nightly rate (cleaning fees set by you). Payments are arranged directly with guests — Stripe Connect integration coming in beta.

Trust & verification

Verified hosts display a badge after ID check. Read reviews on listing pages, message before you book, and never pay outside an agreed method. Report concerns via hello@casa.co.uk.

Still stuck?

Email hello@casa.co.uk or join the waitlist for beta access. Hosts: visit the dashboard for enquiry management.
